Sidebar Aesthetics & Typography

Since we are building a native macOS sidebar, how should the visual aesthetics feel?

1

Classic macOS (Apple Photos Style)

Clean translucent background, standard San Francisco font, and small monochrome SF Symbols (folder icons, calendar icons) next to the dates. Highly professional and native.

2

Modern & Colorful (Finder Style)

Translucent background but with colorful icons for different sections (e.g., blue for folders, colorful tags) and slightly bolder typography for better contrast.
